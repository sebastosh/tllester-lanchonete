Lanchonete.org participated in [Cosmopolis #1 Collective Intelligence](https://c1.cosmopolis.woo.cat/en/) (2017) in Paris at Centre Pompidou and the [10th Bamako Encounters, African Biennale of Photography: Telling Time](https://lanchonete.org/en/2015/10/lanchonete-org-the-10th-bamako-encounters-african-biennale-of-photography-telling-time/) (2015), as well as the 10th and [11th São Paulo Architecture Biennale](https://www.paim.lanchonete.org/2017/12/01/vidas-corpos-com-hiv/)s.

Lanchonete.org is a case example on the University of Coimbra's platform, [Culturizing Sustainable Cities](https://www.ces.uc.pt/projectos/culturizing/?id=10659&pag=13854). In February 2018 [Queer City](https://www.cidadequeer.lanchonete.org/) was featured in Elle magazine (Brasil) after episodes in [Cuiabá](http://cuiaba.lanchonete.org/), Quito and the original in São Paulo.  An [eponymous film](https://www.cidadequeer.lanchonete.org/projetos-projects/ataque/) and [_cidade queer, uma leitora_](http://www.edicoesaurora.com/cidade-queer-uma-leitora/) (from Publication Studio) are by-products of Lanchonete.org's longest-running inquiry. [A training for gardening in irregular spaces](https://mapeamentojardinagemterritorialidade.wordpress.com/hortas-livres-lanchonete-org/) given by Cidades sem Fome and Polish architect Jakub Szczęsny resulted in a do-it-yourself [Guide for Gardening](https://mapeamentojardinagemterritorialidade.files.wordpress.com/2016/05/hortas_livres_a4.pdf) made with the Housing Movement and Aurora Editions. Occupation Sao Joao and Lanchonete.org feature on the WITNESS Blog,  [Mapping Our Materials: The Forced Evictions Advocacy Toolkit Goes Global](https://blog.witness.org/2014/05/mapping-materials-forced-evictions-toolkit/). The [Modes of Democracy (DOX/Prague)](/lanchonete/pdfs/dfd8e8_64bdf2da6f724b39831d6053ca9ac21f.pdf) exhibit catalog; a piece on [_InterArtive_](https://interartive.org/2015/02/lanchonete-org) [Platform for Contemporary Art & Thought](https://interartive.org/2015/02/lanchonete-org); and the Routledge book, [Contemporary Perspectives on Art and International Development](https://www.routledge.com/Contemporary-Perspectives-on-Art-and-International-Development/Stupples-Teaiwa/p/book/9781138024700) all hold the original 'blueprint' for Lanchonete.org ... even if a lot changed over its five-year course.
