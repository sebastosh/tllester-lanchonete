\[:en\]Jakub Szczęsny is a co-founder of [Centrala](http://www.centrala.net.pl/), a design platform from Warsaw, Poland. He operates globally with both architectural and artistic assignments, one of which takes place in frame of the Lanchonete.org project in São João Occupation in São Paulo’s downtown. His project of writer’s studio named Keret House was included in the permanent collection of MoMA in 2013.
