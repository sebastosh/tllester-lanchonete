\[:en\]Adler was born in Teresina, Piauí, Brazil (1986). He lives and works between São Paulo and Teresina. Adler is a visual artist—publication, drawing, installation and a range of collaborations—and independent editor/publisher.  He sees his hybrid working style as a proposition for a contemporary artistic practice comprised of objects as well as cultural and social actions.
