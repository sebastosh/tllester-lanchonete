\[:en\]Steph Yates is an artist, musician, and bookmaker living and working in Guelph, Ontario, Canada. Her practice involves stop-motion animation, installation, kinetic sculpture, printmaking, and mixed media work, which she is currently exploring as an artist in residence at The Boarding House Arts Incubator Program. Since 2013, Yates has been working with Publication Studio Guelph as its studio coordinator, bookmaker, member of the editorial group, and teacher of various hands-on workshops. Yates can often be seen performing in a slew of local bands, and her own musical projects include garage-pop outfit Esther Grey and sparkle-punk duo Cupcake Ductape.\[:\]
