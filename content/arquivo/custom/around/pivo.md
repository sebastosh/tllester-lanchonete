Founded in 2012, PIVÔ is a non-profit cultural organization that operates as a platform for exchange programs and artistic experi- mentation with the objective of proposing critical questioning of the fields of art, architecture, urban planning and other contemporary manifestations.  PIVÔ is a member of the Circuito Centro (http://www.circuitocentro.org).
