\[:en\]Lorena Vicini​ works in institutional relation​s; she is a ​b​ook editor and cultural producer in São Paulo. Her company, Prova3 Agência de Conteúdo specializes in the development of educational and cultural content​. ​She is responsible for the coordination of the project Episodes of the South for the Goethe-Institut, which explores different ways to see the world while asking the question ‘What is the global south?’.  For Lanchonete.org she is responsible for public relations and communications with Brazilians and international partners​. ​Lorena loves carnival and produces  the Bloco João Capota na Alves in São Paulo.\[:\]
