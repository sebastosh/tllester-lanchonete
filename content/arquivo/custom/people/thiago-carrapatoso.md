[:en\]Thiago Carrapatoso is a journalist, specialist in Communication, Arts, and Technology and holds a MA from the Center for Curatorial Studies (CCS) at Bard College (NY). He is a collaborator of the BaixoCentro Movement in São Paulo, Brazil, which tries to reclaim the streets from a civil society perspective. Thiago is the Executive Coordinator of the Digital Program at the Municipal Theater (São Paulo), and along with the REPEP group, helping create a methodology to use heritage education against gentrification.[:]
